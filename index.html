Freejiji
Swipe to discern between free and paid online classified listings.
[more]
  • Daily Challenge Loop: Players evaluate 10 curated local classified listings to guess whether they are offered for free or require payment.
  • Interactive Gesture Controls: Features tactile card-dragging controls for fast-paced decision making.
  • Global Score Distribution: Automatically aggregates and displays real-time score statistics from all players in an interactive chart.
  • Streak Retention Mechanics: Tracks consecutive days played to encourage daily player engagement.
  • Anonymous Authentication: Utilizes silent account creation to sync personal histories and stats securely without a sign-up form.
  • Lockout and Rollover Control: Restricts players to one game per calendar day, resetting automatically at midnight.
  • Local State Preservation: Auto-saves current game progress to local storage, allowing seamless resume if the page is reloaded mid-game.
  • Automated Content Ingestion: Employs an ingestion script to scrape, filter out spam, and schedule daily puzzle decks.
